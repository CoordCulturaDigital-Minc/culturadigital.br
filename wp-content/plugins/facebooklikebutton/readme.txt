== Description ==

The simplest way to add Facebook Like Buttons to your WordPress blog.  Just activate the plugin and and start getting Facebook likes for posts on your blog.  People will be sharing your posts on Facebook before you know it and giving you more traffic and readers on your site.

No complicated setup or mess of settings.  Just activate and you're done!

You have the option to add the Facebook Like Button at the top of your posts or at the bottom.  This setting is available on the "Reading" page of your settings in your WordPress admin panel. It will default to the bottom of the post.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `facebook-like-button.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

OR

Just grab it from the built in plugin directory and choose "Install now", hit ok, then select the "Activate Plugin" link.
