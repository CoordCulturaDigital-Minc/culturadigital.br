===========
INSTALLATION
===========

- Upload the entire "oneroom" folder to your 'wp-content/themes/" folder
- Login into WordPress administration
- Click on the 'Design" tab
- Click on the "One Room" theme thumbnail/screenshot or title

That's it. Go back to the front page of your blog and hit refresh to see your newly installed theme.
